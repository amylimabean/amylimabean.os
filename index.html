About This Bean

BeanOS 1.0

© Amy Lima 2025

This portfolio project is a celebration of the early days of personal computing made me fall in love with the internet in the first place: unpretentious self expression, delightfully simple interactions, and joyful moments of discovery and play.

The intentional blend of retro interfaces with contemporary metaphors serve as an homage to the pioneering patterns that taught us how to click, drag, and navigate our digital world, while honoring the design principles that influence our experiences today.

Importantly, this site is optimized for desktop, like all experiences were when the internet was a place, a room in your house. This digital corner of mine doesn't need to follow you outside of that.

I believe time is the most precious thing we have in this life. I'm deeply honored you're sharing a little bit of it with me, and I hope you enjoy it while you're here.

Created with love, gratitude, nostalgia, and Cursor,
Amy Limabean
